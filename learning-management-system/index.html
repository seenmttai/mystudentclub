Data Sharing Consent Notice

DPDP Act, 2023 Compliant

My Student Club values your privacy. In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 of India, we require your explicit consent to share your professional details.

Key Information Notice:
  • Purpose: To share your uploaded CV (Resume) and profile details with registered companies and recruitment partners to match you with career opportunities.
  • Data to be Processed: Full Name, Email, Phone Number, Educational Details, Work Experience, and uploaded CV/Resume file.
  • Right to Withdraw: You can withdraw this consent at any time, just as easily as giving it, by updating your profile settings on the My Profile page.
  • Unconditional Choice: Providing consent is entirely voluntary. Declining will not affect your course access or learning dashboard, but you won't be considered for active placements.
  • Grievance Redressal: For any queries or concerns regarding your data, contact our Grievance Officer at padam@mystudentclub.com.
